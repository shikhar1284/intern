Biography

Ratan Naval Tata is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist, and former chairman of Tata Sons. A visionary leader, he transformed the Tata Group into a global business empire spanning steel, automobiles, technology, and more. Under his leadership, Tata acquired major global brands like Jaguar Land Rover and Tetley, and launched India's most affordable car, the Tata Nano.

Known for his humility, integrity, and humanitarian values, Ratan Tata has contributed extensively to charitable causes and social development in India. His leadership style emphasizes innovation, ethics, and courage — making him not just a business icon, but an inspiration to millions.
